Research Vision

I aim to develop embedded and reconfigurable systems that integrate machine learning and image processing for adaptive, energy-efficient, and intelligent computation.
My goal is to enable hardware-level intelligence—systems that can sense, analyze, and respond in real time within constrained environments.
I'm particularly interested in how these technologies can advance digital VLSI, electronic system design and bioengineering, from hardware-accelerated image analysis and intelligent sensing to data-driven modeling of biological and environmental systems.
