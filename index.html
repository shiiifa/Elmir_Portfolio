About Me

I'm Mohammed Elmir, an Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate from Ashesi University, Ghana (Cum Laude).

My research interests lie in Bioelectronics, Design of Health Wearables, Embedded Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), Signal Processing, and Hardware Acceleration, with a focus on FPGA-based architectures for low-power, high-efficiency computation at the edge.
